Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies on our website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small pieces of data stored on your device (computer or mobile device) by your web browser. They help us improve your user experience by enabling certain features and functionalities on our website.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the proper functioning of our website. They enable core features like page navigation, accessing secure areas, and more. Without these cookies, some parts of our website may not function correctly.
  • Analytics Cookies: We use analytics cookies to gather information about how visitors use our website. This helps us understand how our website is performing, identify areas for improvement, and optimize user experience.
  • Advertising Cookies: We may use advertising cookies to deliver relevant advertisements and measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns. These cookies may track your online activities across different websites.

How to Manage Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in your web browser settings. Most web browsers allow you to delete cookies, block cookies, or receive a warning before a cookie is stored. Please note that if you choose to block cookies, some features of our website may not work correctly.

Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party service providers to use cookies on our website. These cookies are not under our control, and their use is subject to the respective third-party's privacy policies. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these third-party providers for more information about their cookie practices.

Changes to this Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our cookie practices. Any updates will be posted on this page with the updated revision date.
